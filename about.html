What is THE FIRST CASE?
The First Case CTF has been built specifically for the beginners who are interested in cyber security and hacking. Therefore technologies like cryptography, steganography, hashing is included this CTF. The aim of this is to gain the control of the victim machine and provide the fundamental knowledge of CTFs to the beginners. Here the victim machine, Debian 10 which was specifically configured has been given. Anyone can download the machine and exploit it through their Linux distro. Users cannot be limited to one particular place. So that they have freedom to use any of their choices when it comes to selecting tools which are going to help them while they are gaining the access. If you are facing a trouble, please feel free to contact us.
Who can play THE FIRST CASE
The first case CTF is mainly focused at students who are undergraduates.
